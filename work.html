Kay

Kay is an adorable mini red dachshund. I love taking evening walks with Kay through the streets and having everyone say how adorable she is. People often come to Kay and like to play with her. She has a gentle and loving soul, which can calm you in an instant. She often sits on my lap, stretches his/her paws, and dangles to find the most comfortable position. I think she is the cutest pet anyone could wish for.

Cuddles

Cuddles is a naughty black cat, who likes to mostly chill and relax, sometimes give "cuddles" to its siblings, but only when he feels like it. He likes to enjoy the sun, eat lots of cat treats, climb onto difficult places where no one can reach him and he can observe everything that touches the light. He likes to hide in plain sight, and if he is out of your eye sight, he thinks he is practically invisible (although that is never the case, and it results in most funny situations.)
