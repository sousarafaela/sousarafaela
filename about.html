Hi, I’m Rafaela

and with a camera on my hands, I combine the two passions of life: photography and pets. At some moment in time, I was born in Portugal and throughout my life I was always surrounded by pets - dogs, cats, turtles, fish and birds.

I’m currently based in Tallinn and a proud mother of Kay - a mini red dachshund girl. Welcoming Kay into my family was the turning point in my photography as she made me realize “this is it!”

Photos are a return ticket to a moment otherwise gone. So may your photos be real.

As a photographer, my purpose is to capture the authenticity of each moment I spend with you and your pet. I’m hoping that we cross our paths, and I can tell your story through my lens.
